Open Source and AI Program Office (OSAIPO)

Learn more about the Red Hat Open Source and AI Program Office

Red Hatters have been contributors to open source and community members for open source projects for a very long time. The Open Source and AI Program Office helps Red Hatters find projects, contribute, and lead when the need arises. Explore what we do and why it's important.
